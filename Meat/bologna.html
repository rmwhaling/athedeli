Bologna

Lebanon

Lebanon bologna is a type of smoked and fermented sausage that originated in Lebanon, Pennsylvania, United States. It is made from a blend of beef, sometimes pork, and a unique combination of seasonings, including salt, sugar, black pepper, coriander, and other spices. The sausage is typically fermented and then smoked, which gives it its distinct flavor and aroma. Lebanon bologna can be found in various forms, including sweet, sweet and sour, and regular (also known as original or plain). It is often enjoyed sliced thinly and served cold as a snack or appetizer. It has become popular not only in Pennsylvania Dutch country but also in other parts of the United States.
